Cupcakes

You will need:

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 150g
Caster Sugar 150g
Eggs 3
Self-Raising Flour 150g
Butter (For Frosting) 100g
Icing Sugar 200g

Method:

  • Beat together butter and caster sugar until pale and creamy.
  • Add in eggs one at a time while continuing to mix.
  • Add in self-raising flour and mix to combine
  • Divide mixture between 12 cupcake cases and bake at 180C for 10-12 minutes.
  • Once cooked leave to cool before frosting
  • To make the frosting beat the butter until soft and pale and slowly add in the icing sugar to avoid lumps. Flavour and colour as preferred.
