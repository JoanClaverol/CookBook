Tortellini soup

You will need:

Ingredient Quantity
Olive oil 1 tbs
Garlic 1-2 cloves
Shallot 1
Vegetable or meat broth 4 cups
Oregano 1/2 tsp
Basil 1/2
Salt 1/4 tbs
Pepper or hot pepper 1-2 dashes
Tortellini (meat) 10 ounces
Raw spinach leaves 6 1/2 cups
INSTRUCTIONS

1. In a large pot add the oil, minced garlic and chopped shallot, cook for approximately 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
2. Then add the broth, tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil (break up the tomatoes with a fork), stir to combine. Simmer for 20 minutes, then add the tortellini, cook as indicated on the package, one minute before they are cooked, add the chopped spinach and stir gently to combine. Taste for salt (add if necessary). Serve immediately, top with flaked or grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy!
